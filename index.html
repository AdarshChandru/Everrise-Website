About Us

Everrise Engineering has been a cornerstone in the electrical engineering sector, renowned for our robust and innovative solutions tailored for commercial needs. Our mission is to deliver exceptional service and cutting-edge products, ensuring each project contributes positively to our clients' operational success.

One-stop Solution for All Your Panel Needs

We provide comprehensive electrical solutions, from design to installation and maintenance.

Trusted Service

Our reputation for reliability and excellence makes us a trusted partner in the industry.

A-Grade License

We hold an A-grade license, ensuring the highest standards of safety and quality in all our projects.

Innovative Solutions

Our vision drives us to be industry leaders, consistently expanding our expertise in electrical solutions while maintaining sustainability and efficiency.

Since our inception in 2003, Everrise Engineering has consistently met and exceeded the needs and expectations of our clients. We specialize in company shutdowns, offering rapid service and effective solutions. Our team comprises highly skilled professionals dedicated to catering to each of our clients' unique requirements.

At Everrise Engineering, we boast a state-of-the-art factory equipped with advanced machinery, including press brakes, shearing machines, ironworkers, laser cutting machines, turret punches, and welding kits. All fabrication is carried out in-house, ensuring that we maintain high standards of quality and precision.

Our factory operates 24/7 to meet the demands of our clients, with our team on-site to assess situations and coordinate with our factory team to commence work seamlessly. This synchronized approach ensures that projects are completed efficiently and to the highest standards.
